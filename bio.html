Biography

Intro

Rita Jessa is a 30-year-old lady, currently living and working full-time as a nurse in Ireland. She is originally from the Philippines and had been a nurse for quite some time. She loves music and meeting up with friends, and two words to describe this young lady is she’s very religious and inspirational. The Almighty had always been the centre of her life and she never fails to see situations with a silver lining. Her positive outlook in life draws a lot of people from different walks of life.

In her free time, she would usually read very interesting books or get in touch with her family and friends in the Philippines. She definitely is a people person and treasures her friends and family greatly.

Background

She was born in Iloilo, a province which is located in the centre of the Philippine archipelago. It was given the title of “Queen City of the South” for being the Philippine’s second city, after Manila. Growing up in Iloilo, she has acquired this charm and sweetness that is very native among the people of Iloilo.

She migrated to Ireland to seek for greener pastures and upon coming to Ireland, she has continuously served in the church and participated in the service as a member of the choir.

Career

Music has always had been one of her passion. She has been gifted with this angelic voice that will soothe and comfort your ears. She started singing at an early age and despite having no formal training, her natural talent developed and has been giving joy to a lot of her acquaintance and family.

She decided to pursue her music career as a calling. She believes that her voice will move a number of peoples’ heart and encourage them to be more thankful and appreciative of life. Her songs are currently ‘covers’ only but in the future, she hopes to deliver original compositions that would delight, inspire and bring people closer to the Almighty.

"She is a very inspiring lady. Never she forgets to thank the Almighty for all the blessings she receives"

-Michael Pineda
